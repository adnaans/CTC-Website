About Mithra

MITHRA is a Sanskrit word meaning 'friendship'. Friendship to MITHRA means extending a warm welcome to the physically and mentally challenged children. MITHRA came into being on 19th March 1977. A unique feature of MITHRA is that it offers the differently abled a variety of services all under one roof: Special School, Occupational & Physiotherapy, Vocational Training, Residential facility, Psychiatry Department apart from an OPD that is open to the public.
